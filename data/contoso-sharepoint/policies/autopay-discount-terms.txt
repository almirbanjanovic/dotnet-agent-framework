Autopay Discount Terms
Contoso Telecom — Policy Document
Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Document ID: POL-007

OVERVIEW

Subscribers enrolled in automatic payment (autopay) receive a $5.00 monthly discount on their service bill.

ENROLLMENT

Autopay can be enabled through:
- The self-service portal at myaccount.contoso.com
- The Contoso mobile app
- Contacting Customer Support at 1-800-CONTOSO

The discount takes effect on the next billing cycle after enrollment.

PAYMENT FAILURE

If an autopay payment fails:
- The $5.00 discount is forfeited immediately
- The discount is reinstated only after two consecutive successful automatic payments
- During the reinstatement period, the customer is billed at the standard (non-discounted) rate

ELIGIBLE PAYMENT METHODS

Autopay can be configured with:
- Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- Debit card
- ACH bank transfer
- Apple Pay or Google Pay

CANCELLATION

Customers may cancel autopay at any time. The discount is removed effective the next billing cycle after cancellation.

For questions regarding this policy, contact Customer Support at 1-800-CONTOSO.
