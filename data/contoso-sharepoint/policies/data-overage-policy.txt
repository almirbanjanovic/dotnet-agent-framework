Data Overage Policy - Mobile Plans
Contoso Telecom — Policy Document
Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Document ID: POL-001

OVERVIEW

When subscribers exceed the monthly data allowance on a capped plan, an overage fee is billed in 1-GB increments at the rate specified in the subscriber's service agreement.

OVERAGE RATES

- Mobile Plan - Essential (5GB cap): $10.00 per GB over the monthly allowance
- Fiber Internet - Basic (500GB cap): $5.00 per GB over the monthly allowance
- Fiber Internet - Pro (1TB cap): $5.00 per GB over the monthly allowance
- Unlimited plans (Mobile Premium, Fiber Ultimate): No overage applies; speeds may be deprioritized after 50GB during network congestion

RETROACTIVE PLAN UPGRADE

Customers may switch to a higher-tier plan retroactively within 15 days of the invoice date to have overage charges re-rated. The upgrade will be pro-rated based on the remaining billing cycle. To request a retroactive upgrade, the customer should contact support or use the self-service portal at myaccount.contoso.com.

EXCLUSIONS

Loyalty tier discounts do not apply to overage fees. Promotional credits are applied before overage calculations. International roaming data usage is billed separately under the International Roaming policy.

NOTIFICATIONS

Customers receive automated alerts at 75%, 90%, and 100% of their data allowance via SMS and email. Customers who have disabled notifications are still subject to overage charges.

For questions regarding this policy, contact Customer Support at 1-800-CONTOSO.
