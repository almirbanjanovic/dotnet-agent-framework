Service Reliability SLA - Credit Matrix
Contoso Telecom — Policy Document
Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Document ID: POL-006

OVERVIEW

Contoso is committed to maintaining high service availability. When network outages or quality degradations exceed defined thresholds, account credits are automatically applied to affected customers.

CREDIT MATRIX

Outage or degradation duration determines the credit amount, calculated as a percentage of the customer's monthly service fee:

- 1 to 3 hours: 10% of monthly fee credited
- 3 to 24 hours: 25% of monthly fee credited
- More than 24 hours: Full month fee credited (100%)

ELIGIBILITY

Credits apply to:
- Unplanned network outages affecting the customer's service area
- Quality degradations where measured speeds drop below 25% of the subscribed tier for the stated duration
- Equipment failures on Contoso-owned infrastructure

Credits do NOT apply to:
- Scheduled maintenance (customers are notified at least 48 hours in advance)
- Customer equipment issues (router, modem, device)
- Third-party service disruptions (content providers, DNS, etc.)
- Force majeure events

APPLICATION

Credits are applied automatically within 2 billing cycles of the qualifying event. Customers who believe they are eligible for a credit that was not applied can contact support for manual review.

BUSINESS CUSTOMERS

Business Internet - Enterprise subscribers with SLA 99.99% have enhanced credit terms as specified in their individual service agreements.

For questions regarding this policy, contact Customer Support at 1-800-CONTOSO.
