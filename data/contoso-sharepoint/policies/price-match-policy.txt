Price Match Policy
Contoso Outdoors

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Overview

Contoso Outdoors offers a price match guarantee to ensure our customers get the best value. If you find a lower price on an identical item from an authorized competitor, we will match that price.

2. Eligibility

To qualify for a price match:

- The item must be identical (same brand, model, size, and color)
- The competitor must be an authorized retailer (not a marketplace third-party seller)
- The competitor's price must be currently available and verifiable online or in print
- The item must be in stock at the competitor's store or website
- The request must be made within 14 days of your Contoso Outdoors purchase

3. Exclusions

The following are excluded from price matching:

- Clearance, closeout, or liquidation prices
- Membership-only pricing (e.g., warehouse club prices)
- Coupon or promotional bundle pricing
- Prices from auction sites, marketplace sellers, or unauthorized retailers
- Used or refurbished items
- Pricing errors on competitor websites
- Gift-with-purchase offers

4. How to Request a Price Match

1. Contact customer service via chat or phone
2. Provide your Contoso Outdoors order number
3. Share a link or screenshot of the competitor's lower price
4. A customer service agent will verify the price and process the adjustment

5. Refund of Difference

If a price match is approved:
- The difference is refunded to the original payment method within 3-5 business days
- If the purchase was made with a gift card, the difference is credited back to the gift card

6. Limitations

- Price matching is limited to one request per item per customer
- Contoso Outdoors reserves the right to limit or refuse price match requests at its discretion
- Price matches cannot be combined with other promotions or discounts
