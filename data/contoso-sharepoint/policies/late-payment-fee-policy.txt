Late Payment Fee Policy
Contoso Telecom — Policy Document
Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Document ID: POL-003

OVERVIEW

A late fee is assessed if an invoice remains unpaid beyond the due date specified in the customer's billing statement.

FEE CALCULATION

The late fee is calculated as 1.5% of the outstanding balance per month, beginning the day after the due date. The fee is compounded monthly for continued non-payment.

GRACE PERIOD

There is no automatic grace period. However, customers who contact support before the due date may request a one-time extension of up to 7 days without incurring a late fee.

FEE WAIVER

Customers who demonstrate financial hardship or account errors can have late fees waived at management's discretion. A waiver request must be submitted within 30 days of the late fee being assessed.

SERVICE IMPACT

Accounts with payments more than 30 days past due risk service suspension. Accounts more than 60 days past due may be downgraded to a lower service tier until the balance is cleared. Accounts more than 90 days past due may be sent to collections.

AUTOPAY INCENTIVE

Customers enrolled in autopay receive a $5 monthly discount and avoid late fees entirely, provided sufficient funds are available in their payment method.

For questions regarding this policy, contact Customer Support at 1-800-CONTOSO.
