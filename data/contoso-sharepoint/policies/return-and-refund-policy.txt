Return and Refund Policy
Contoso Outdoors

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Return Window

Contoso Outdoors accepts returns within 30 calendar days from the date of delivery. The delivery date is determined by the carrier's delivery confirmation. Items returned after 30 days are not eligible for a refund or exchange.

2. Condition Requirements

To be eligible for a return, items must meet the following conditions:

- Unused and in original condition, with all tags attached
- In the original packaging (if applicable)
- Free of dirt, stains, pet hair, and odors
- Footwear must not show sole wear beyond indoor try-on

Items that have been used on the trail, washed, or altered are not eligible for return. However, items with manufacturing defects are covered under our Warranty Policy regardless of use.

3. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:
- Clearance or final-sale items (marked at checkout)
- Gift cards
- Food items, water treatment chemicals, and fuel canisters
- Customized or monogrammed gear
- Undergarments and swimwear (for hygiene reasons)

4. How to Initiate a Return

Customers can initiate a return by:
1. Contacting customer service via chat or phone
2. Providing the order number and reason for return
3. Receiving a prepaid shipping label via email
4. Packing the item securely and dropping it off at any carrier location

5. Refund Processing

Once the returned item arrives at our warehouse and passes inspection:

- Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days
- The refund is issued to the original payment method
- Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to a Contoso Outdoors error (wrong item shipped, defective product)
- Customers receive an email confirmation when the refund is processed

6. Exchanges

Customers who wish to exchange an item for a different size or color should refer to the Exchanging a Product procedure. Exchanges are subject to stock availability.

7. Holiday Extended Returns

Orders placed between November 15 and December 31 have an extended return window through January 31 of the following year.
