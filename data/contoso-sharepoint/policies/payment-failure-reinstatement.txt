Payment Failure and Reinstatement Rules
Contoso Telecom — Policy Document
Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Document ID: POL-005

OVERVIEW

This policy describes the process and rules governing automatic payment failures and subsequent service reinstatement.

GRACE PERIOD

If an automatic payment attempt fails (e.g., insufficient funds, expired card), the account enters a 10-day grace period before service suspension. During this period:
- The customer receives notifications via email and SMS on days 1, 5, and 9
- All services remain fully active
- No late fees are assessed during the grace period

SERVICE SUSPENSION

If the balance remains unpaid after the 10-day grace period:
- Service is suspended (outgoing calls/data blocked; incoming calls still received)
- A reconnection fee of $25.00 is added to the account balance
- The customer must pay the full outstanding balance plus the reconnection fee to restore service

RECONNECTION FEE WAIVER

The reconnection fee is waived for first-time payment failures within a rolling 12-month window. Subsequent failures within the same 12-month period are subject to the full reconnection fee.

HARDSHIP PAYMENT PLAN

Customers who cannot pay the full balance may enroll in a hardship payment plan (see Financial Hardship Payment Plan Procedure). Enrollment in a hardship plan reinstates service immediately.
