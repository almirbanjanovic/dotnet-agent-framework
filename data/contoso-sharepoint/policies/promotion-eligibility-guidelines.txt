Promotion Eligibility Guidelines - Loyalty and New Subscribers
Contoso Telecom — Policy Document
Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Document ID: POL-004

OVERVIEW

Contoso promotions reward both loyal participants and newly enrolled subscribers. This document outlines the eligibility criteria for all active and recurring promotional offers.

LOYALTY-BASED PROMOTIONS

Loyalty-based promotions scale with membership levels:
- Gold members qualify for the most generous plan discounts (up to 100% off upgrades)
- Silver members qualify for moderate discounts (up to 20% off select services)
- Bronze members qualify for entry-level promotional offers

Loyalty level is determined by account tenure and payment history. Members must maintain their loyalty level at the time of promotion redemption.

NEW SUBSCRIBER OFFERS

New subscriber offers typically require at least a six-month lapse since any prior Contoso subscription. New subscriber status is verified at the time of enrollment. "Win-back" promotions may have different lapse requirements and are communicated separately.

GENERAL ELIGIBILITY RULES

- Some promotions require a minimum contract term (typically 12 months)
- A clean billing history (no outstanding balance) is required for most promotions
- Availability can vary by region and product line
- Terms may limit combining multiple offers simultaneously
- Promotional pricing applies for the stated duration only; standard rates resume after the promotional period ends

VERIFICATION

Eligibility is verified automatically at enrollment. Customers who believe they qualify but are denied can contact support for manual review.

For current promotions, visit contoso.com/promotions or contact Customer Support at 1-800-CONTOSO.
