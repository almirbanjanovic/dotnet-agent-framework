Loyalty Program Terms
Contoso Outdoors — Trail Rewards Program

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Overview

The Trail Rewards Program is Contoso Outdoors' loyalty program. Members earn points on every purchase and unlock tier-based benefits as they shop. Membership is free and automatic when you create a Contoso Outdoors account.

2. Earning Points

- Members earn 1 point per $1 spent on eligible purchases
- Bonus point events may offer 2x or 3x points on select categories
- Points are credited to your account within 24 hours of order delivery
- Points are not earned on gift card purchases, shipping fees, or taxes
- Returned items result in deduction of the associated points

3. Loyalty Tiers

Tier status is determined by total points earned in a rolling 12-month period:

| Tier | Points Required | Benefits |
|------|----------------|----------|
| Bronze | 0 - 499 | Earn 1 pt/$1, access to member-only promotions, birthday discount (5%) |
| Silver | 500 - 1,499 | All Bronze benefits + free standard shipping, early access to sales, 10% birthday discount |
| Gold | 1,500 - 3,999 | All Silver benefits + free expedited shipping, exclusive Gold promotions, 15% birthday discount |
| Platinum | 4,000+ | All Gold benefits + free overnight shipping, 20% birthday discount, dedicated support line, annual gear credit ($50) |

4. Tier Evaluation

- Tier status is evaluated on a rolling 12-month basis
- If a member's points drop below their current tier threshold, they have a 3-month grace period before being moved to the lower tier
- Tier upgrades take effect immediately upon reaching the qualifying threshold

5. Redeeming Points

- Points can be redeemed for discounts on future purchases
- 100 points = $5 off
- Minimum redemption: 100 points
- Points expire 24 months after they are earned if not redeemed
- Points cannot be transferred between accounts or converted to cash

6. Promotion Eligibility

Many promotions have a minimum loyalty tier requirement. When a promotion lists a minimum tier (e.g., "Silver and above"), only members at that tier or higher are eligible. The agent should check the customer's loyalty tier against the promotion's minimum tier requirement.

7. Tier Hierarchy for Eligibility

When checking promotion eligibility, the tier hierarchy is:
Bronze < Silver < Gold < Platinum

A customer qualifies for a promotion if their tier is equal to or higher than the promotion's minimum tier. For example:
- A Gold member qualifies for promotions requiring Bronze, Silver, or Gold
- A Bronze member only qualifies for promotions requiring Bronze
