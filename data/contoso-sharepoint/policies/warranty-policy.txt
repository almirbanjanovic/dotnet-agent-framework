Warranty Policy
Contoso Outdoors

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Overview

Contoso Outdoors stands behind the quality of every product we sell. This warranty policy covers manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship for products purchased directly from Contoso Outdoors (online or in-store).

2. Contoso Warranty Coverage

Products sold under the Contoso Outdoors brand carry a 2-year limited warranty from the date of purchase. This warranty covers:

- Seam failures and stitching defects
- Zipper malfunctions not caused by misuse
- Delamination of waterproof membranes
- Broken buckles, clips, or hardware that fails under normal use
- Fabric tearing under normal use conditions

3. Manufacturer Warranty

Third-party brand products sold through Contoso Outdoors are covered by the original manufacturer's warranty. Contoso Outdoors will assist customers in filing claims with the manufacturer when possible. Manufacturer warranty periods vary by brand and are listed on the product page.

4. What Is NOT Covered

This warranty does not cover:
- Normal wear and tear (fading, abrasion, pilling)
- Damage caused by misuse, abuse, or modification
- Damage from improper care (e.g., machine washing a dry-clean-only item)
- Cosmetic damage that does not affect functionality
- Products purchased from unauthorized resellers

5. Filing a Warranty Claim

To file a warranty claim:
1. Contact customer service with your order number and a description of the defect
2. Provide clear photos of the defect
3. A customer service agent will review the claim within 3 business days
4. If approved, you will receive a prepaid shipping label to return the defective item

6. Resolution Options

For approved warranty claims, Contoso Outdoors will:
- Replace the item with the same product (if in stock)
- Offer a comparable replacement if the original is discontinued
- Issue a store credit for the original purchase price if no replacement is available
- Repairs may be offered for certain items (tents, backpacks) at Contoso's discretion

7. Proof of Purchase

A valid proof of purchase is required for all warranty claims. This can be:
- Order confirmation email
- Order number from your Contoso Outdoors account
- Original receipt (in-store purchases)
