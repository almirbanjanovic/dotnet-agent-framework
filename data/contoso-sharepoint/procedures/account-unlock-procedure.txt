Account Unlock Procedure - Verification Steps
Contoso Telecom — Procedure Document
Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Document ID: PROC-001

PURPOSE

This procedure describes how to unlock a customer account that has been locked due to multiple failed login attempts or a detected security anomaly.

WHEN AN ACCOUNT IS LOCKED

Accounts are automatically locked after 5 consecutive failed login attempts. A security anomaly (e.g., login from an unusual location or device) may also trigger a lock.

VERIFICATION STEPS

1. Confirm the customer's identity using at least two of the following:
   a. Last 4 digits of their Social Security Number
   b. Date of birth
   c. Billing address on file
   d. Last payment amount and date

2. If verification succeeds, send a temporary unlock code to the customer's registered contact method (email or SMS on file).

3. The customer enters the temporary code on the login page. The code expires after 15 minutes.

4. Upon successful entry, the account unlocks and the customer is prompted to reset their password.

FAILED VERIFICATION

If the customer cannot pass identity verification:
- Do NOT unlock the account
- Direct the customer to visit a Contoso retail location with government-issued photo ID
- Note the failed verification attempt in the account security log

SUSPECTED FRAUD

If the interaction suggests the caller may not be the account holder:
- Do NOT unlock the account
- Escalate immediately to the Fraud Department at ext. 7700
- Document the interaction details in the security log

POST-UNLOCK RECOMMENDATIONS

After unlocking, recommend the customer:
- Reset their password immediately
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Review recent account activity for unauthorized changes

For questions regarding this procedure, contact the Security Team at ext. 7700.
