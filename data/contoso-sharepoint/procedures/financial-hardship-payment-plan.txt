Financial Hardship Payment Plan Procedure
Contoso Telecom — Procedure Document
Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Document ID: PROC-004

PURPOSE

This procedure describes how subscribers who are temporarily unable to pay their balance in full may request a hardship installment plan.

ELIGIBILITY

To qualify for a hardship payment plan, the subscriber must:
- Have an outstanding balance of at least $50.00
- Provide proof of income disruption or comparable documentation (e.g., job loss letter, medical documentation, government assistance enrollment)
- Not have an active hardship plan already in place
- Not have defaulted on a previous hardship plan within the last 12 months

APPLICATION PROCESS

1. The customer contacts Customer Support and requests a hardship payment plan.
2. The agent verifies the customer's identity and reviews the account balance.
3. The customer provides or submits documentation of financial hardship.
4. The documentation is reviewed by the Billing Team within 3 business days.

PLAN TERMS

Once approved:
- All late fees are frozen for the duration of the plan
- The outstanding balance is split into up to 6 equal monthly payments
- Service is reinstated immediately (if previously suspended)
- The customer must maintain autopay for the duration of the plan
- Missing a hardship plan payment results in immediate plan cancellation and the full remaining balance becoming due

PLAN COMPLETION

Upon successful completion of all installments:
- The account is returned to normal standing
- Late fee history is cleared from the account
- The customer's loyalty tier is preserved

For questions regarding this procedure, contact the Billing Team at ext. 4400.
