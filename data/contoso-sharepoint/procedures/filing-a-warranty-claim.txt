Filing a Warranty Claim
Contoso Outdoors — Internal Procedure

This procedure outlines how to process a warranty claim for a defective product.

1. Initial Assessment

When a customer contacts us about a potentially defective product:

a. Gather Information
   - Order number and purchase date
   - Product name and description of the defect
   - Ask the customer to provide photos of the defect
   - Ask how the item was used (to determine normal use vs. misuse)

b. Determine Warranty Type
   - Contoso Outdoors brand products: 2-year limited warranty from purchase date
   - Third-party brand products: Refer to manufacturer warranty period
   - If outside the warranty period, inform the customer and offer alternative options (repair services, discount on replacement)

2. Photo Review

Review the customer's photos to determine:
   - Is this a manufacturing defect? (seam failure, zipper malfunction, delamination, broken hardware)
   - Is this wear and tear? (fading, abrasion, pilling — not covered)
   - Is this damage from misuse? (cuts, burns, improper washing — not covered)

If unclear from photos, request the item be sent in for inspection.

3. Create Support Ticket

   - Create a support ticket with category "product-issue"
   - Set priority to "high" for safety-related defects (e.g., tent pole failure, buckle failure on harness)
   - Set priority to "medium" for functional defects (zipper, seam, waterproofing failure)
   - Include photos and the customer's description in the ticket

4. Claim Decision

Within 3 business days of receiving the claim (or the physical item if inspection is needed):

   - APPROVED: Proceed to resolution (step 5)
   - DENIED: Contact the customer with explanation. Offer a discount on a replacement as a goodwill gesture.

5. Resolution for Approved Claims

Offer the customer one of the following, in this order of preference:

a. Replacement
   - Same product, same size/color if in stock
   - If the original is out of stock or discontinued, offer a comparable replacement
   - Ship the replacement with expedited shipping at no cost

b. Store Credit
   - Issue store credit for the original purchase price
   - Credit does not expire
   - Use when no suitable replacement is available

c. Repair (tents and backpacks only)
   - Offer to repair the defective item if feasible
   - Repairs typically take 2-4 weeks
   - Ship a prepaid label for the customer to send the item

6. Return of Defective Item

   - For replacements and store credit, the customer must return the defective item
   - Generate a prepaid shipping label
   - In some cases (low-value items under $30), waive the return requirement

7. Documentation

   - Update the support ticket with the resolution and outcome
   - Close the ticket once the replacement is shipped or credit is issued
