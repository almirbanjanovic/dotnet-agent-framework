Return Policy and Process
Contoso Telecom — Procedure Document
Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Document ID: PROC-003

PURPOSE

This procedure describes how customers may return purchased products and the conditions under which returns are accepted.

ELIGIBILITY

Customers may return eligible products within 30 days from the purchase date. The following conditions apply:
- Proof of purchase (receipt, order confirmation, or order ID) is required
- Products must be in like-new condition with minimal wear
- All included accessories, manuals, and packaging must be returned
- Custom-configured or personalized items are not eligible for return

RESTOCKING FEE

A restocking fee of 15% of the purchase price may apply if:
- The product shows signs of damage beyond normal inspection handling
- Accessories or packaging are missing
- The product has been used beyond initial setup and testing

The restocking fee is waived if the return is due to a Contoso error (wrong item shipped, defective product, etc.).

RETURN PROCESS

In-Store Returns:
1. Bring the product and proof of purchase to any Contoso retail location
2. A store associate will inspect the product and process the return
3. Refund is issued to the original payment method within 3–5 business days

Mail-In Returns:
1. Contact Customer Support or visit myaccount.contoso.com to initiate a return
2. A prepaid shipping label will be emailed within 24 hours
3. Pack the product securely and attach the shipping label
4. Drop off at any authorized shipping location
5. Inspection occurs upon receipt at the Contoso warehouse
6. If approved, refund is issued within 7–10 business days

SHIPPING COSTS

Return shipping is free when:
- The return is due to a Contoso error
- The product is defective

For all other returns, shipping costs (typically $8.95) are deducted from the refund amount.

For questions regarding this procedure, contact Customer Support at 1-800-CONTOSO.
