BEFORE YOU TRAVEL

International roaming allows you to use your mobile plan outside your home country. Before departing:

1. Check if your plan includes international roaming:
   - Mobile Plan - Premium: Free roaming in 50+ countries included
   - Mobile Plan - Essential: Roaming available as an add-on ($10/day in 100+ countries)

2. Activate roaming at least 24 hours before crossing the border to ensure provisioning across partner networks. Same-day activation is best-effort and may incur delays up to 6 hours.

3. Download offline content (maps, documents, entertainment) before you leave to reduce data usage abroad.

ROAMING RATES

Standard roaming rates (without a travel pass):
- Voice calls: $1.50/minute
- SMS: $0.50/message
- Data: $15.00/GB

Travel Pass ($10/day):
- Unlimited calls and texts
- 1GB high-speed data per day (reduced speeds after)
- Available in 100+ countries

MANAGING DATA ABROAD

- Use WiFi when available to save mobile data
- Disable automatic app updates and cloud photo sync
- Enable data usage alerts in your Contoso app to monitor consumption
- Turn off cellular data for apps you don't need while traveling

SUPPORTED COUNTRIES

Visit contoso.com/travel for the full list of supported countries and region-specific rates. Coverage varies by partner network availability.

RETURNING HOME

Roaming is automatically deactivated when you return to your home network. Any travel pass charges stop on the day you return. If roaming charges appear on your next bill and you were not traveling, contact support immediately.

For questions, contact Customer Support at 1-800-CONTOSO.
