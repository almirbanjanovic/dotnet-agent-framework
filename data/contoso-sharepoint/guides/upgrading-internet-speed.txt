Upgrading Your Internet Speed
Contoso Telecom — Customer Guide
Document ID: GUIDE-004

AVAILABLE SPEED TIERS

Contoso offers the following internet speed options:

- Fiber Internet - Basic: 100 Mbps ($49.99/month)
- Fiber Internet - Pro: 500 Mbps ($79.99/month)
- Fiber Internet - Ultimate: 1 Gbps ($119.99/month, unlimited data)
- Business Internet - Enterprise: 10 Gbps ($299.99/month, dedicated line)

HOW TO UPGRADE

Self-Service:
1. Log in to your account at myaccount.contoso.com
2. Go to Services > Internet
3. Click "Upgrade Plan"
4. Select your new speed tier
5. Review the price change and confirm

By Phone:
Call 1-800-CONTOSO and ask to upgrade your internet plan. Our agents can also check for available promotions that may reduce the upgrade cost.

WHAT TO EXPECT

- Speed upgrades typically take effect within 24 hours
- You may need to restart your router for the change to apply
- Your next bill will reflect the pro-rated difference for the remainder of the current billing cycle
- The new monthly rate takes effect on the following full billing cycle

DOWNGRADING

To downgrade your speed tier, follow the same steps above and select a lower tier. Downgrades also take effect within 24 hours. Note that some promotional pricing may not be available if you downgrade and later re-upgrade.

LOYALTY DISCOUNTS

Gold loyalty members may qualify for a free speed upgrade promotion. Check current promotions at contoso.com/promotions or ask a support agent.

For special upgrade offers, call us at 1-800-CONTOSO.
