COMMON TEXT MESSAGING ISSUES

If you're experiencing problems sending or receiving SMS or MMS messages, follow these troubleshooting steps.

MESSAGES NOT SENDING

1. Check your signal strength — at least 2 bars are needed for reliable messaging
2. Verify your account is active and in good standing at myaccount.contoso.com
3. Restart your phone (power off, wait 10 seconds, power on)
4. Check if you have sufficient balance (for prepaid plans)
5. Try sending a message to a different number to determine if the issue is recipient-specific

MESSAGES NOT BEING RECEIVED

1. Restart your phone to re-register with the network
2. Check if your message inbox is full — delete old messages to free space
3. Verify that the sender has the correct phone number
4. Check if you have accidentally blocked the sender's number
5. Ensure your phone's date and time are set to automatic

MMS (PICTURE/VIDEO) MESSAGES FAILING

1. Ensure mobile data is enabled (MMS requires a data connection, WiFi alone is not sufficient)
2. Check that MMS messaging is enabled in your phone's messaging settings
3. Verify your APN settings are correct (contact support if unsure)
4. Reduce the file size — videos over 1MB may fail to send

SERVICE OUTAGES

Larger outages or system maintenance windows are usually posted on the service status page at status.contoso.com. Check there before contacting support to see if there is a known issue in your area.

IF ISSUES PERSIST

Open a support ticket with the following information:
- Timestamps of failed messages
- Phone numbers involved (sender and recipient)
- Screenshots of any error messages
- Your device make, model, and operating system version

This helps our technical team investigate more efficiently.

For assistance, contact Customer Support at 1-800-CONTOSO.
