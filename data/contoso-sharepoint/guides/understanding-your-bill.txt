YOUR MONTHLY BILL EXPLAINED

Your Contoso bill is issued on the same date each month. Here's what each section means:

ACCOUNT SUMMARY

The top of your bill shows:
- Your account number and billing period
- Previous balance and any payments received
- New charges for the current period
- Total amount due and due date

MONTHLY SERVICE CHARGES

This section lists recurring charges for each active subscription:
- Internet service (based on your speed tier)
- Mobile plan (based on your data/voice plan)
- TV streaming (based on your channel package)
- Home security monitoring
- Bundle discounts (if applicable)

ONE-TIME CHARGES

Non-recurring charges may include:
- Equipment fees (router, modem, set-top box)
- Installation or technician visit fees
- Early termination fees
- Reconnection fees

USAGE-BASED CHARGES

Variable charges based on your actual usage:
- Data overage fees (if you exceed your plan's data cap)
- International roaming charges
- Premium content or pay-per-view purchases

TAXES AND REGULATORY FEES

Government-mandated taxes and fees that Contoso is required to collect. These vary by location and are not set by Contoso.

CREDITS AND ADJUSTMENTS

Any credits applied to your account:
- Promotional discounts
- Service outage credits (per the Service Reliability SLA)
- Billing dispute adjustments
- Autopay discount ($5.00/month)

PAYMENT OPTIONS

- Autopay: Set up automatic payments to never miss a due date and receive a $5 monthly discount
- Online: Pay at myaccount.contoso.com
- Mobile app: Pay through the Contoso app
- Phone: Call 1-800-CONTOSO to pay by phone
- Mail: Send a check to the address on your bill

Payment is due by the date shown on your bill. See the Late Payment Fee Policy for details on late payments.

View your bill online at myaccount.contoso.com or in the Contoso mobile app.
