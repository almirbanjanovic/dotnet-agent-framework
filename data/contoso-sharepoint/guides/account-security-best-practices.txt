PROTECTING YOUR ACCOUNT

Your Contoso account contains personal information, payment details, and service configurations. Follow these best practices to keep it secure.

PASSWORD REQUIREMENTS

- Use a strong, unique password (at least 12 characters with uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols)
- Never reuse passwords from other websites or services
- Update your password every 90 days
- Never share your login credentials with anyone

TWO-FACTOR AUTHENTICATION (2FA)

Enable 2FA for an extra layer of security:
1. Log in at myaccount.contoso.com
2. Go to Account Settings > Security
3. Click "Enable Two-Factor Authentication"
4. Choose your verification method (SMS, email, or authenticator app)
5. Follow the setup prompts

With 2FA enabled, you'll need both your password and a verification code to log in.

MONITORING YOUR ACCOUNT

- Review your account activity regularly for unfamiliar changes
- Check for unauthorized subscription modifications or address changes
- Enable login notifications to receive alerts when your account is accessed from a new device or location

WHAT TO DO IF YOU SUSPECT UNAUTHORIZED ACCESS

1. Change your password immediately
2. Review recent account activity for unauthorized changes
3. Contact our Security Line at 1-800-CONTOSO-SEC
4. If your account is locked, follow the Account Unlock Procedure

WHAT CONTOSO WILL NEVER DO

- We will NEVER ask for your password via email, phone, or text message
- We will NEVER send you a link asking you to "verify" your account credentials
- We will NEVER ask for your full Social Security Number

If you receive any communication asking for this information, it is not from Contoso. Report it to our Security Team immediately.

For security questions, contact 1-800-CONTOSO-SEC.
